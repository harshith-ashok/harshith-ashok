I’m a ☕ coffee-sipping programmer who enjoys turning ideas into reliable digital systems.

My work spans a wide range of technologies from **web applications and enterprise systems**
to **embedded hardware and automation**. On the web side, I build complete applications
across both **frontend and backend**, focusing on clean architecture and long-term maintainability.

For interfaces, I use **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript** along with modern frameworks like  
**React, Vue, and Angular** to create responsive, intuitive user experiences.

On the backend, I work with **Python and Node.js**, using frameworks such as  
**Django, Flask, Express, and FastAPI** to design APIs, business logic, and data pipelines.

I also build **full-blown enterprise applications** using **Frappe**, crafting custom apps,
workflows, and data models for real-world business use cases.

Lately, I’ve been diving deep into **Agentic AI systems and LLM-powered applications** —
building AI agents, document-grounded decision systems, and automation that integrates
directly into production workflows.
